🏛️ Team Operating Model Architect

A guided tool to design your team's purpose, boundaries, operating model, and leadership engagement approach.

How To Use This Tool

This is not a slide generator. It is a thinking framework.

Work through the sections in order:

  • Why do we exist?
  • What value do we provide?
  • What do we own?
  • How does work move?
  • How do decisions happen?
  • How should others engage with us?

Your answers automatically save in your browser.
