🏛️ Team Operating Model Builder

A guided framework to intentionally design how your team operates.
Purpose:

This tool helps you move from:

"We have a team and a lot of good people doing important work..."

to:

"We have a clear purpose, defined boundaries, decision rights, operating rhythm, and leadership model."

Recommended approach: Do not try to complete everything at once. Think through each room. Capture imperfect answers. Refine over time.

Progress

Built as a thinking tool — not a template. Design first. Document second.
