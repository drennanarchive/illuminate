Monthly Leadership Touchpoint

PMR-Inspired Portfolio & Governance Review | Draft Concept for Discussion
Purpose:
Create a predictable forum for strategic alignment, portfolio awareness, and leadership engagement across team priorities.

Focus: Where we are → Where we are going → Where leadership guidance is needed

Team Provides Leadership

  • Portfolio visibility
  • Progress and key changes
  • Persistent blockers
  • Strategic signals and observations
  • Upcoming priorities

Leadership Provides Team

  • Organizational context
  • Priority alignment
  • Strategic direction
  • Decisions and guidance
  • External insights
Monthly Discussion Flow
1. Context
What changed around us?
2. Status
Where are we?
3. Decisions
What needs attention?
4. Signals
What are we seeing?
5. Future
Where are we going?
This IS:
  • Strategic
  • Portfolio-focused
  • Decision-oriented
  • Leadership-enabling
This is NOT:
  • Tactical status reporting
  • Task tracking
  • Replacement for operational meetings
  • Detailed PM review
Initial recommendation: Monthly cadence | Leadership + Team | Lightweight pre-read supported discussion
