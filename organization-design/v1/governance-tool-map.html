Four versions of the team operating model design tool

All four walk through the same nine areas. v3 has the most output generators, including a leadership review package. v31 is a lighter rebuild of v3 with fewer outputs. Builder adds an explicit "what already exists" diagnosis and a checkbox tracker, but has no document generator — notes only. Builder v2 restores a document generator with example placeholder text.

Three versions of leader-rhythm design

use this team-cadence-tool.html
— Leadership_Operating_Rhythm_Workshop.html
— Leader_Touchpoint_Canvas.html
All three help design or redesign the rhythm between a leader and their team. The Workshop is the deepest — six steps, framed around a role that just expanded. The Cadence Builder is a lighter five-step worksheet with autosave, built around one "key loop" (what goes up, what comes down, how escalation works). The Canvas is the lightest — a single-pass brainstorm with no step sequence, fully covered by the Cadence Builder's first and last steps.
