💧 Shower Tips for Glued Lashes

  • Avoid heavy steam the first 24 hours.
  • Don’t let shower spray hit lashes directly.
  • Warm water is better than very hot water.
  • Pat dry gently.
  • Use cool blow dryer if desired.
  • Spoolie once lashes are completely dry.
